Our Story

Growing Together

Kugaya's Garden & Landscaping is a trusted name in Mombasa's gardening community. Based in Mtwapa ATC, we grow and sell a wide variety of quality plants for homes, hotels, offices, and outdoor environments.

Beyond plants, we provide professional landscaping and gardening services — from full garden design and grass installation to tree arrangement and rock gardens. Whether you're looking for a single plant or a complete landscape transformation, our experienced team is here to help.

Happy people create beautiful spaces. Our team works together with care, respect, and love for plants and nature.
